AI Fairness and Law Project

Using AI to Increase Fairness by Improving Access to Justice

This project seek to increase social fairness by using AI to improve public access to justice.

Many AI tools are already streamlining legal workflows, however, such tools do not reach members of the public and legal service practitioners except through a commercial paywall that prohibits many from accessing them. As a result, these tools are usually available only to a very select few who can afford them.

In FAI project, we leverage the recent advances in machine learng, natural language processing(NLP), legal argument modeling, computational statutory interpretation that enpowers users to access high quality legal information with much lower cost.
